About Us!

Nykita and I met during our junior and sophomore years at Red Mountain High School wayyyyy back in 2006. Nykita had sent an email to me (Derek), applying to be the singer in my band. After that, we were pretty much inseperable.

Before we finally met up at school, we would stay up chatting all night on MySpace and AOL Instant Messenger and we both quickly became one of eachother's "Top 8." Finally, after a few days of trying to meet up at lunch and somehow failing to find eachother, we met between classes. Next thing we knew... though that band thing never really did take off.

Eleven years later and we're finally tying the knot, on the anniversary of our first date: Friday the 13th of October. We've had a lot of ups and downs but always come out stronger and we can't wait to share the rest of our lives together, with our beautiful little girl, Alexa!
